Frequently Asked Questions

What is SlideScope?

SlideScope is a professional desktop application for viewing microscopy images. It supports CZI (Zeiss), ND2 (Nikon), and SVS (Aperio) file formats and runs on both Windows and macOS. A 2-day free trial is available, after which an annual license is required.

How much does SlideScope cost?

SlideScope costs $49 per year for an annual license. A 2-day free trial is available with no credit card required. The license includes full feature access, free updates, and email support.

What microscopy file formats does SlideScope support?

SlideScope currently supports CZI files from Zeiss microscopes, ND2 files from Nikon imaging systems, and SVS files for whole slide imaging from Aperio scanners.

Can I view Z-stack and time-lapse images?

Yes! SlideScope includes built-in navigation for Z-stacks and time series data. Use the slider controls and arrow keys to navigate through different slices and timepoints in your microscopy data.

Does SlideScope work on Mac and Windows?

SlideScope is cross-platform software that works on Windows 10/11 and macOS 10.14 or later. Native applications are available for both operating systems.

How do I open microscopy files in SlideScope?

Simply drag and drop your CZI, ND2, or SVS files directly into the SlideScope window. No complex import process required - it's that easy!

Can SlideScope handle large high-resolution images?

Yes! SlideScope is optimized for viewing large, high-resolution microscopy images including whole slide scans. It includes smooth zoom and pan controls for detailed examination.

Is SlideScope suitable for clinical use?

SlideScope is excellent for research and educational purposes. For clinical diagnostic use, please consult your institution's requirements for medical imaging software validation and certification.
