## ğŸ Free Trial Strategy

### Why 14 Days is Optimal

**Too Short (7 days):**
- Not enough time to evaluate
- Users feel rushed
- Lower conversion rates

**Too Long (30 days):**
- Users forget about it
- Less urgency to convert
- Higher abandonment

**14 Days is Perfect:**
- 2 weeks = enough time to test thoroughly
- Creates urgency (weekend + weekdays)
- Industry standard
- High conversion rates (12-18%)

### Trial Optimization

**Day 1:** Welcome email + onboarding guide
**Day 3:** Feature highlight email ("Did you know...")
**Day 7:** Check-in email ("How's it going?")
**Day 10:** Reminder email (4 days left)
**Day 13:** Last chance email (24 hours + discount code)
**Day 15:** Post-trial survey + re-engagement offer

**Expected Trial-to-Paid Conversion: 15-20%**
